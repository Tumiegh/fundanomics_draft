About Fundanomics

Fundanomics is committed to using education as a tool to shape future citizens who will safeguard democratic gains, value voting, and become proactive members of society.

Our Vision

To use education and literature to shape minds today who will lead tomorrow.

Our Mission

To create educational tools and programs that orient the next generation of patriotic and responsible citizens.

The Purpose of Fundanomics

The primary purpose of Fundanomics is to ensure that as many children as possible have access to quality education programs and tools, igniting reading and learning for meaning.

Fundanomics Democracy Pack

A series of books for children between 7 and 12 to teach them about the basic concepts of South African democracy.

Ubuntu

Democracy

The Constitution

The Economy

Patriotism

The Books

The books follow the adventures of two young characters, Themba and Tammy, as they learn about democracy, patriotism, the economy, the constitution, and ubuntu with teacher Miss Khumalo.
