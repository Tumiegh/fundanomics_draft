About Fundanomics

Fundanomics is committed to using education as a tool to shape future citizens who will safeguard democratic gains, value voting, and become proactive members of society.

Our Vision

To use education and literature to shape minds today who will lead tomorrow.

Our Mission

To create educational tools and programs that orient the next generation of patriotic and responsible citizens.

The Purpose of Fundanomics

The primary purpose of Fundanomics is to ensure that as many children as possible have access to quality education programs and tools, igniting reading and learning for meaning.

Project Pilot

The Fundanomics project team has identified Gauteng Province as a pilot area for the distribution of the democracy pack. The idea is to integrate the learning process with the benefits of understanding democracy.

Our Literacy Project

  • One of the interventions by Fundanomics is a literacy programme called BRIGHT BOOK BEES where the team collects and distributes books for children around Gauteng communities. We also run reading Fridays at local libraries and community centers.
  • Resources are a major challenge in our disadvantaged communities, hence public libraries become an important space for children to read, engage, and freely interact with their counterparts. Public libraries are also safe spaces with technological access when possible.
